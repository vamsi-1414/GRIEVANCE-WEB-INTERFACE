WELCOME TO GRIEVANCE WEB!

                    
                    A PLATFROM FOR POSTING YOUR ISSUE OR ANY SUGGESSTION REAGARDING ANY SECTOR . 
                    
                    HERE IS HOW TO ACCESS THE WEBSITE:

                    
 
                        ACCOUNT CREATION,LOGIN SECTION.
                    

                    INITIALLY YOU HAVE TO SIGN IN TO CREATE AN ACCOUNT 
                       
                        TO SIGN IN:
                        YOU NEED TO PROVIDE THREE PARAMETERS : USERNAME,PASSWORD,ROLL NO.
                    AFTER SIGN IN (ONCE ACCOUNT HAS CREATED YOU CAN LOGIN)

                    LOGIN TO YOUR ACCOUNT BY ENTERING THE USERNAME AND PASSWORD 
                     THAT YOU HAVE PROVIDED WHILE SIGN IN PHASE.

                    AFTER ENTERING THE DETAILS 

                    CLICK ON SUBMIT.

                    

                        REPORTS:CREATION AND VIEWING.
                    

                    YOU WILL REDIRECT TO YOUR HOME PAGE WHILE ADDITONALLY SHOW REPORTS OPTION.

                    TO VIEW REPORTS THAT YOU HAVE POSTED EARLIER
                        CLICK ON VIEW REPORTS OPTION.
                        IT WILL DISPLAY THE REPORTS YOU HAVE PROVIDED.

                    
TO ISSUE A REPORT


                    CLICK ON "ISSUE A REPORT" OPTION 
                        THEN SELECT THE SECTOR THAT YOUR ISSUE DEPENDS ON.
                        (SELECT OTHERS OPTTION IF YOU DID NOT FIND THE REQUIRED SECTOR).

                        THEN ENTER THE ISSUE IN THE PROVIDED TEXT FIELD.

                        THEN CLICK ON SUBMIT.

                        IN THIS WAY YOU CAN SUCCESSFULLY POST AN ISSUE.
